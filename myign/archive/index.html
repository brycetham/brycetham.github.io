MyIGN Archive

Between 2011 and 2016, I wrote about video games on IGN under the username Battlestriker123. Below is an archive of my works, retrieved in January 2018 after IGN announced that it would stop hosting user blogs later that year. The posts below are missing community comments and have gone through minor changes for archival purposes but otherwise remain in their original form. You can read a continuation of my works on The Battlestriker Blog.
